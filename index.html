HISTORY

Rick and Morty is an American adult animated television series created by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon for Adult Swim. The series follows the adventures of an alcoholic scientist, Rick, and his easily influenced grandson, Morty, who pass the time between family domestic life and intergalactic space travel. The series currently has six seasons.
Throughout its five seasons, the animated series that can be seen on HBO Max has shared several details about Rick C-137's past; However, it is later said that his memory was fabricated, therefore its origin becomes a mystery. In the fifth installment of "Rick and Morty," Evil Morty does a full scan of Rick C-137's brain, allowing us to learn everything about Rick's life. Screen Rant shared the biggest reveals.
